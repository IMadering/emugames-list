This is a DVD released in 2008 to promote the release of Little King's Story (Ōsama Monogatari, 王様物語) for the Nintendo Wii.
The DVD contains about 40 minutes of footage, which includes:
1. Two Japanese trailers for Little King's Story, one of which is narrated in English.
2. A 30-minute Let's Play of the game's opening by two Japanese players.
3. Three trailers for other games published by Marvelous: Valhalla Knights, Arc Rise Fantasia, and Muramasa: The Demon Blade.

Only 20 of these DVDs were produced and given away by Famitsu.
