Baby Einstein - World of Discoveries is an educational interactive game designed for the youngest children, developed by VTech for the V.Smile Baby gaming system, which targets kids aged 9 months to 3 years.
Throughout the game, children become familiar with everyday objects, colors, shapes, sounds, and basic concepts.
