An ancient hazardous game.
Try it — maybe fortune will smile on you.

Your goal is to get more points. There are two game modes: mode of throw and mode of combinations selection. To switch between the modes, use <Tab>.
To select dice, move the pointer by <Left/Right arrows> and press <Select> or <Ins>. To throw selected dice, press <Enter>. If you don't need to throw the dice, go to the combinations mode, select the combination by <Left/Right arrows> and press <Enter>.
The combination must be completed in three throws. The combination can be used only once during the game.

Scoring:
Aces Total Aces only
Twos Total Twos only
Threes Total Threes only
Fours Total Fours only
Fives Total Fives only
Sixes Total Sixes only
3 of a Kind Total of all Dice
4 of a Kind Total of all Dice
Full house 25 points (3 of a Kind + 2 of a Kind)
Small Straight 30 points (Any 4 Consecutive Dice)
Large Straight 40 points (Any 5 Consecutive Dice)
Total Total of all Dice
Pocker 50 points (5 of a Kind)

Press <Esc> to exit.
