Are you ready to take a chance with Poker Slot Machine? Try now!

Pair (two cards of the same rank from Jack) – 1.
Two pairs (two cards of one rank and two cards of another rank) – 2.
Three of a kind (three cards of the same rank) – 3.
Straight (five cards in sequence) – 4.
Flush (five cards of the same suit) – 5.
Full house (three of a kind and one pair) – 8.
Four of a kind (four cards of the same rank) – 25.
Straight flush (a sequence of five cards in the same suit) – 50.
Royal flush (a sequence ten to Ace in one suit) – 1,000.

Controls
<Down arrow> – to decrease a stake.
<Up arrow> or <Select> – to increase a stake.
<Left arrow> – to make a minimum stake.
<Right arrow> – to make a maximum stake.
Press <Enter>.

Select card for discarding using <Left/Right arrows> and press <Select> when you want to select all cards you want. To discard, press <Enter>. The Gain shows how many points you win. Press <Enter> to refresh the bank.
Press <Esc> to exit.
