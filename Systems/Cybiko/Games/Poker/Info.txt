The goal is to break the bank in this popular card game for two players. A win depends not only on the player's cards, but also on how the player makes the bets, bluffing and exerting psychological pressure on the partner.

Control
<Down arrow> - to decrease a bet.
<Up arrow> or <Select> - to increase a bet.
<Left arrow> - to set a "drop."
<Right arrow> - to place a maximum bet.
To change the cards, select each card by <Left/Right arrows> and press <Select>.
Then press <Enter>.
Press <Esc> to exit.

The classic combinations of cards in a poker hand are:
Pair - two cards of the same rank.
Two pairs - two cards of one rank and two cards of another rank.
Three of a kind - three cards of the same rank.
Straight - five cards in sequence.
Flush - five cards of the same suit that are not in sequence.
Full house - three of a kind and one pair.
Four of a kind - four cards of the same rank.
Straight flush - a sequence of five cards in the same suit.
The highest hand wins the bank.
The game can be played until one of the players has the points in the pocket.
