Intellectual shooting game. Outgun your enemy or be destroyed!

If you play against CYBIKO, you can fire when you see a flag above your gun. Your gun controls are active when it's your turn.

Controls
Use <Left/Right arrows> to rotate the barrel.
Use <Up/Down arrows> to select the initial speed of the missile. The length of the bar in the lower part of the screen indicates its velocity.
Press <Enter> to fire the missile.
Press <Esc> to quit the game.
