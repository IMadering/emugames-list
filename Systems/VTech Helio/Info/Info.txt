The VTech Helio is a personal digital assistant (PDA) released by VTech in the late 1990s.

Technical Specifications
CPU: Toshiba TMPR3912AU, 75 MHz, 32-bit RISC
Storage (RAM): 8 MB SDRAM
Storage (Flash): 2 MB
Audio: built-in microphone and speaker
Display: 160 x 160 resolution (59 x 59 mm), 16-level grayscale LCD
OS: VT-OS, which is VTech's Palm OS clone
