Vince's new version of the classic 1970's BASIC game, Star Trek.

Apple Star-Trek is a sci-fi strategy game that was written in Integer BASIC for the Apple-1 by Robert J. Bishop and released by McPheters, Wolfe & Jones. It is based on a text-based game originally created in 1971.
