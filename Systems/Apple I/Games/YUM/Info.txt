This is a computer version of a game called Yum, similar to games like Yahtzee, Yacht, and Generala.

Rules of the Game:
Each player rolls five dice up to three times, then uses the result of the roll to get points in a certain category.

Rounds:
The game consists of 12 rounds, during which each player strives to get the most points in each category.

Winner:
The winner is the player with the most points at the end of the game.

Number of Players:
This version supports up to three players, who can be either human or computer opponents.
