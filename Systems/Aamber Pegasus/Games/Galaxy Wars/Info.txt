Load from Cart:
After the monitor boots up, press the first letter of the program on the cartridge: G

How to Play
The game will first ask you for settings.
Difficulty (No. of asteroids): Enter 1 for easy (fewer asteroids) or 2 for difficult (many asteroids).
Number of torpedoes (No. of torpedoes): Enter 1 or 2.
Game speed (Speed of game): Enter 1 for slow or 2 for fast.

After you select your options, the battle scene will appear on the screen.
Objective: Your main goal is to pilot your spaceship to the top of the screen while avoiding asteroids and bombs. Colliding with an enemy at the top will earn you points.

Controls
K - Left
L - Right
, - To move upward, press and hold; releasing the key will slow your upward movement.
N - restart game.

Rules
You start with three spare platforms. If you are hit by a bomb or collide with an asteroid, you will lose one platform.
You can continue to play as long as you have platforms remaining.

Scoring
Successfully colliding with an enemy will increase your score by 200–900 points, and the enemy will disappear.
If you manage to destroy all the enemies, they will all reappear, and you will gain one extra platform.

Standard Settings
Asteroids: 1 
Torpedoes: 2 
Speed: 2
