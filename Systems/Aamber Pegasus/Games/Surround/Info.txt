A simple game where you need to avoid obstacles.
You can speed up the emulator by 200% to get acceptable speed!

Controls
1 - Down
2 - Up
7 - Right
8 - Left

Load from Cassette:
Insert the cartridge with FORTH and after the monitor boots, press: F
Then type the command: LOAD and press Enter
Turn on the tape playback.
After the boot is complete, type the command: GO
