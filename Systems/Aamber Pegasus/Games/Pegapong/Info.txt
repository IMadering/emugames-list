A simple Pong game.
Implementation in BASIC, but unfortunately it works very slowly.
You can speed up the emulator by 400% to get acceptable speed!

Controls
Left paddle
Q - Up
A - Stop
Z - Down

Right paddle
P - Up
L - Stop
, - Down

Load from Cassette:
Insert the cartridge with BASIC and after the monitor boots, press: B
Then type the command: LOAD and press Enter
Turn on the tape playback.
After the boot is complete, type the command: RUN
