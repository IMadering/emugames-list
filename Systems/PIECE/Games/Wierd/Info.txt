Wierd (version 1.26)

This is an action-packed shooter.

Use regular shots and charged bombs to defeat enemies.
If your ship comes into contact with enemy bullets or the enemy itself, you will die.

Wired Bomb:
When the gauge in the upper left corner of the screen is full, you can shoot. When it hits an enemy, it explodes. The explosion can also destroy enemy bullets.

Controls
Pilot movement: D-pad
Normal shot: B button
Wired bomb: A button
