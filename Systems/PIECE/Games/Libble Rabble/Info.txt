This game is very similar to Libble Rabble (Namco, 1983).

Draw a line around the mushrooms to erase them.
When all the Mashlines are removed, the stage is clear.

Touching the Hoblin (Wizard) is a mistake.
Three mistakes will result in the game over.

When the scissors touch the line, it becomes straight again.
Touching the stock is not a mistake.

Use the up, down, left, and right buttons to move the bar (left arrow).
Use the START, B, SELECT, and A buttons to move the bar (right arrow).

Naoyuki Sawa
