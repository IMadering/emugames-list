The meaning of this game is to climb up and earn points.
This game is a continuation of the previous game "CLIMBER".
In this game, a variety of items appear.
Although some items that appeared in the previous game will be retained, many new items will also be introduced.
In addition, this time, enemy characters will appear. Enemies will try to knock the player down or destroy him in different ways.
Moreover, many new systems have been introduced, and we are sure that you will like the game even more than the previous part.
