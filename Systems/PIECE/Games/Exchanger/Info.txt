Exchanger Simulation

This game is very similar to Money Idol Exchanger (c) 1997 FACE.

During the game:
- Left and Right buttons: Move the exchanger.
- B button: Collect coins.
- A button: Flips a coin.
- Down button: Immediately draws the next line.

Same rules as the original Money Idol Exchanger.

Please glue the coins together and exchange them.
- Five 1 yen coins glued together make a 5 yen coin.
- Two 5 yen coins glued together make a 10 yen coin.
- Five 10 yen coins glued together make a 50 yen coin.
- Two 50 yen coins glued together make a 100 yen coin.
- Five 100 yen coins stuck together make a 500 yen coin.
- When two 500 yen coins stick together, they disappear.

You can have as many of the same type of coin as you want at the same time.
You cannot have different types of coins at the same time.

When exchanging coins, the stuck coins
The bottom and rightmost coins will change.

The coins on the field will fall at regular intervals.
You can also force it down with the down button.
If a coin goes beyond the bottom row, the game is over.
