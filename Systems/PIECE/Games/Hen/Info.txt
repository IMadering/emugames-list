Hen (ver. 2.1.023)

Move the hen and the chickens and return the hen to her coop.
This is a puzzle game where you have to complete levels.

Controls
Direction keys - Move the chicken and chicks in the direction you press.
However, it will not move unless both the chicken and chick can move in that direction.
[A], [B] - Rotate the chicken 90 degrees clockwise and counterclockwise around the chicken.
However, the chicken will not move unless the destination is somewhere it can fly through.
Terrain other than the destination does not matter.
[START] Restart the current stage from the beginning.
[SELECT] You can select a stage you have already played.
