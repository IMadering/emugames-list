How to play
Please check all boxes except "○ with notch on top" and "○ with notch on bottom".
Please do not check "○ with notch on top" or "○ with notch on bottom".
Compete to see who can check the most boxes correctly in 30 seconds.

Controls
Left button: Moves the cursor forward.
Right button: Moves the cursor to the next position.
Up and Down buttons: Not used.
Button A: Check the box. Clicking on an already checked circle again will uncheck it.
Button B: Cancel the remaining time and start scoring immediately.

Scoring system
If you check any circle except "Circle with notch on top" or "Circle with notch on bottom", you will get 1 point.
If you mark "○ with notch on top" and "○ with notch on bottom" you will get -5 points. Everything else does not count.
