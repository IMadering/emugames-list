VAX11

This is a score shooter where all characters are drawn using ASCII characters.

Control your own ship "VT100" and defeat the approaching enemies.
If you are hit by an enemy bullet or the enemy itself, you will die.
Score as many points as possible before time runs out.

A button - fires a shot.
B button - activates a bomb.
Up, down, left, right buttons - move your ship.
