Xylophone, xylophone, Mako training!
Use the arrow keys to move the target and the A/B buttons to hit the xylophone sticks and defeat ♪!

Control Keys:
Move the target left and right.
A, B Buttons - Hit the xylophone sticks.
START - Press and hold for a certain period of time to reset.
SELECT - "Activate your secret skill." While the target is active, it becomes larger.

Your health will be restored by 1 unit for every 5 ♪ destroyed (when ♪ is defeated or disappears off-screen).
