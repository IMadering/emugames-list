This game is very similar to "Panel de Pon" (1995, Intelligent Systems).

How to play
Swap the left and right panels and line them up in rows of three or more to remove them.
If you do nothing, the panel will gradually rise.
When the panel reaches the top, the game ends.

Controls
Up, Down, Left, Right buttons Move the cursor.
Button: Swap panels.
Button B: Raises the panel quickly.
