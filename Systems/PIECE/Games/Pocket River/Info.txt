Pocket River (version 1.03)
This is what they call "Othello".

During the game, the player and the computer take turns placing black and white stones.
Use the up, down, left, and right arrow keys to move the cursor, and press the A button to place a stone.
If there is no room for a stone, press the B button.
Now it is the computer's turn.
Conversely, if the computer cannot place a stone, the turn still goes to the player.
Neither the player nor the computer can place a stone, or all the stones have already been placed.
If you do this, the result of the game will be displayed and you will be returned to the title screen.

If a "↓" icon appears under the message on the right side of the screen,
