A barrage of bullets from all directions. Barrage reactor.

Control your ship in the center of the screen and destroy the barrage of bullets that appear around you.

Lasers can destroy barrage sources and bullets.
Collect fragments when you destroy them. Each item is unique.

Your ship will be destroyed if it comes into contact with a bullet or source of fire.
If all your ships are destroyed, the game is over.
For every 3000 points you will receive an additional plane.
