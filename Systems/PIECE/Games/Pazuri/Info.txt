This game is very similar to Pazuri (Metro, 1995).
This is Pazuri, an arcade puzzle game from Metro.

How to play
- Remove all the fish and bubbles from the screen.
- When three or more of the same fish are next to each other on the surface of the ocean, they disappear.
- Bubbles disappear when they rise to the surface of the water.
- The sea level will decrease every time you perform the operation a certain number of times.
・If the fish touches the current at the bottom of the ocean, the game is over.

Controls:
Left and Right buttons: Move the boat.
Button: Place the fish.
B Button: Change the fish.
SELECT Button: Return to the title screen.
