3D Maze

This is a game that displays a maze in 3D.
Move from the start (coordinates (0,0)) to the goal (coordinates (125,85)).

When you start, a maze will be created.
Press the START or A button to go to the movement screen.

On the movement screen, press the UP or A button to move forward, LEFT to turn left, RIGHT to turn right, and DOWN to turn back.
Also, pressing the B button will display the entire maze (map).
SELECTION is complete.

Your current location will flash on the entire maze (map) screen.
When you press the B button, a cross line will appear on the screen indicating your current location.
Press the START or A button to go to the movement screen.

When you score a goal, the screen will display "GOAL!".
Press START to return to the beginning.
