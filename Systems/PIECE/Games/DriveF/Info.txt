DriveF (Wind drive)

This is a 3D racing game that focuses on speed rather than looks.
Race through the city and mountains!

How to play
Control the steering wheel and brake (automatic acceleration) to drive the car along the road, avoiding collisions with the walls on the left and right.
The remaining time is displayed in the upper left corner. When it reaches 0, the game ends.
The remaining time is added by passing checkpoints. After four checkpoints, the fifth one becomes the target.

Controls
Control keys: steering wheel
A button: brake
Acceleration is automatic, acceleration occurs when you release the A button.
START key: pause
SELECT key: sound on / off
