Use the mysterious robot "BIT" to destroy other robots.

Defeat enemies and reach the goal. Enemies can be defeated by jumping on them or hitting them with boxes.

Controls:
・Left and Right buttons - Accelerate "BIT".
・A button - Jump "BIT". Also, depending on how long you press during the jump, you can adjust the height of the jump.
・B button - When a box appears in front of "BIT", pick it up.
