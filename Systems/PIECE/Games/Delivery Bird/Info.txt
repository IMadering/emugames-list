Delivery Bird (version 1.1.37)

This is a puzzle game where you have to complete several levels by controlling a bird and delivering luggage to the specified location.

Rules
* The bird can move freely left, right, and down, one square at a time.
* The bird can climb one square if there is a wall or luggage directly below it.
* However, when holding luggage, the bird must support itself on the surface below it.
* The bird can only push one piece of luggage at a time.
* However, when holding luggage, the bird cannot push it.
* The bird can grab and carry luggage that is directly below it.
* If the bird does not hold luggage and there is no support underneath it, the luggage will fall.
* The stage is considered completed when all the "decorated" luggage has been delivered to all the racks.
* However, the luggage must remain stationary on the rack.
(There are exceptions to the above rules for some types of baggage. For details, see the section "Types of baggage".)

Controls
[←][→] Move one space left or right.
[↓] Decrease height by one space.
[↑] Jump one space.
[A] Grabs luggage directly below you. Press again to release.
[B] When pressed, the birds and luggage will disappear, and you can check the location of the mailbox.
[SL] Return to level selection.
Press during level selection to return to the main screen.
