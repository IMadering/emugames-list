Fukuro is an action game where you get beat up.

The figure in the center of the screen is you.
Arrow keys: Move
A button / B button: Punch

Enemies will gather around you to attack you.
Please knock them down.

If you are hit by an enemy, your stamina will decrease.
Your stamina is displayed as a bar at the bottom of the screen.
When your stamina runs out, the game ends.
If you stand still without moving or hitting,
Your stamina will gradually recover.
