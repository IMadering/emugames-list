Simple Submarine

This game is very similar to Depth Charge (c) SEGA.

Rules
Destroy as many submarines as possible within the allotted time of 99 seconds.
The game ends when the time runs out or three battleships are destroyed.
If the time runs out, 100 points will be added for each remaining battleship.

Controls:
Cursor keys to move the battleship left and right.
A and B buttons to drop bombs left and right.
