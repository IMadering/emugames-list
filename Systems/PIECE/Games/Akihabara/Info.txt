Akihabara SHT!

How to do it:
A - Shot (hold to shoot continuously).
B - Change (Mariko -> Fumi -> Cynthia).
SELECT - Crash (effects vary depending on character).
START - Pause.

Recovery items:
Soft Ice Cream - Recovers 4 HP.
Oden Can - Recovers 10 HP.
Dolnesand - Fully recovers HP.
Takoyaki - Resurrects the dead.

Bonuses:
Nurse Uniform - Increases HP limit.
Chinese Clothes - 100 EXP gained.
Maid Uniform - Increases accidents.

Experience Points:
LV2 - Accident UP.
LV3 - Shot speed increased.
LV4 - Rapid Fire UP.
LV5 - BOOSTER.
