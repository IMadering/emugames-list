Simple G-Wars

This game is very similar to Galaxy Wars (© Universal).

Control the rocket and launch it at the UFO.
Hitting the meteorite or the beam is considered a miss.
(Hitting the UFO is not considered a miss.)

The closer you get to the center of the UFO, the higher your score.
The highest scores are 300, 150, 100, and 50.

Controls
On the ground:
Move the turret left and right.
Press the A button to launch the rocket.

In the air:
Move the rocket left and right.
Press the A button to accelerate.
